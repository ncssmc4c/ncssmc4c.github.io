About Us

Code for Charity is a group of students from the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics who are dedicated to giving back to our community through software development. We create engaging websites and mobile apps for charity organizations, all for free. We are passionate and knowledgeable about computer science, programming, and software development, and we are committed to applying these skills for the improvement of our community.

About our School

NCSSM is a public residential high school in Durham, North Carolina that specializes in the STEM fields of study. It is ranked the #1 public high school in North Carolina, and inspires students to become future leaders in innovation.
